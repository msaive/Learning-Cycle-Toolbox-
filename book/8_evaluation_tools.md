# ‚ö™ TEAM EVALUATION TOOLS :: I want to do a team evaluation

### **What are these tools?**

These tools provide suggestions for evaluation formats that can be used to evaluate the group dynamics, collaboration and work processes in the learning team.

### **Why are these tools useful?**

For learning teams to work effectively, everyone should feel safe and comfortable in the group. Also effectiveness of work processes, and steps to take the collaboration to a next level are aspects to review and reflect on together on a regular basis. These evaluations may also provide individual students with input for further growth as collaborator, leader or communicator (for example: how take your role as discussion leader to a next level).

### **When to use these tools?**

These tools are specifically designed to be used during evaluation moments. You are free to together determine when to do this; we recommend to evaluate team functioning at least once every period and sooner whenever needed.

## **A tool to evaluate learning team functioning**

----------

#### **How to use this tool?**

This tool can be used to evaluate team functioning within the learning team and/or within 1-on-1 meetings with the LTC.  
The Blobtree is a visual tool that you -  **students and coaches alike**  - can use to start a conversation about group functioning.

----------

#### **Suggested questions**

Use the blobtree picture on the next page to print out and discuss. All kind of questions can be asked, for example:  
¬∑ Which blob depicts you in our learning team, and why?  
> Do others agree? Do they see you differently?  
¬∑ Which blob according to you, best depicts our LTC, and why?  
> Does the group agree? Are there different ideas?  
¬∑ To what extent does the blobtree represent our team as a whole?  
> What analogies do you see? In what areas can we grow, as a team?  
¬∑ Adapt the questions to your liking and needs, be creative!
